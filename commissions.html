Answered

Common Inquiries

How long does a commission take?

Anywhere from two weeks for a small graphite piece to two months for a large painting. I will give you a firm estimate during our first conversation.

What kind of reference photos work best?

Clear, well lit photographs in natural light are ideal. The more I have to work from, the more alive the final piece will feel. Don't worry if you only have one, we can make it work.

Can I commission a piece as a gift?

Yes, and it is one of my favorite kinds of work. Let me know the occasion and the timeline so we can plan ahead.

Do you ship internationally?

I do. Shipping is calculated separately based on size and destination, and every piece is packaged with great care.

What if I'm not sure what I want?

That is perfectly fine. Reach out anyway. Some of the best pieces have begun as just a feeling or a memory.
